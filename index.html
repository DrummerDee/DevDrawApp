Instructions before drawing

You can change the width of your line by adjusting the range slider.

You can change the color of your line by using the color picker.

You can undo your most recent drawn line by clicking the undo button.

You can clear your drawning altogether by clicking the clear (x) button.

Now that you've read the instructions, you're ready to draw! Have Fun :)
